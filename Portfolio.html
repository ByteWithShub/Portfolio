About

WhoI Am

I spend a lot of time building and reworking things. Most of it isn’t perfect the first time. That’s kind of the point. You adjust, rebuild, and try again. Eventually, something holds.

AI Engineer.

Hey, This is Shubhangi and welcome to my corner of the web!

I build end-to-end systems that take unstructured or structured data and turn it into outputs that can actually be used. My work focuses on connecting the full pipeline from data ingestion and preprocessing to model integration, evaluation, and final outputs rather than treating models as standalone components.

I’ve developed RAG pipelines for question answering by designing chunking strategies, managing embeddings, and improving retrieval quality to keep outputs consistent across different queries. In parallel, I’ve trained and evaluated computer vision models for classification tasks, focusing on dataset preparation, augmentation, and improving generalization rather than just optimizing metrics.

On the robotics side, I’ve worked with ROS2-based simulation environments, building systems that connect perception, control, and learning. This includes handling real-time data flow, interpreting sensor inputs, and ensuring that decisions made by the system translate into meaningful actions.

Across all of this, my focus is on system behavior, how components interact, where failures occur, and how to improve consistency across different inputs. I evaluate beyond single metrics, analyze failure patterns, and refine systems so they remain stable and interpretable over time.

The goal is not just to generate outputs, but to make them understandable and actionable. If a system cannot be explained, trusted, or used effectively, it is not finished.

Process

How IWork

01

Understand

I start at the real problem, not the decorative version of it. The right model on the wrong question is still wrong.

02

Experiment

I compare, test, challenge assumptions, and rebuild. I like systems that earn confidence instead of borrowing it.

03

Structure

When projects get noisy, I bring shape back to them. Good systems and good teamwork are rarely separate problems.

04

Refine

If it cannot be explained, trusted, or used, it is unfinished. I care about clarity as much as performance.

05

Ship with care

What I build is meant to hold up in the real world, under pressure, around edge cases, and beyond the demo moment.
